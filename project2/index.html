I’m fun, and chaotic, and I’ll think about you when I sleep every night.

TO BE UNDERWATER

I like how you say things, or the way you used to say things. I like how it changes. I like how you blink a lot, when something is in your eye. And then you get it out, and you look at me, like something happened in the interim.

I’m almost home, the fog is rolling in as I disappear farther into the trees. I place myself inside of something soft and considerate, and I lift my heavy body, and step off, and I close my eyes until i’m home. And then I sleep, and I wait.

| OPEN ME, I'M HERE |

I can feel the night as it finds it’s way into my room. I can hear it, how it pushes me. There’s a pressure that builds in my ears, and for a moment i’m stuck in the sound. And then I come back, a little contorted. Undone.

I’m turning over the idea in my head. I could walk there. It’d be dark, on the freeway overpass. I could yell and nobody would hear me. I could turn it over in my head, over and over, If I wanted to.

I like disappearing into the noise. The idea of it comforts me. There’s always a train I could take. The same one comes every night. I could follow it anywhere. If I really needed to, I could.

I grant strangers their wishes. I watch them yell back at me, and I stand there, confused, like I’ve been drinking too much. I feed people. I let them spill me, undo me.

I let them feed on me. I let them cut me open and pick me apart, siphon oil, clean up a bit. I fill my body with toxins and I let them sink me in the river, an old record, with less and less to say every day.

| CALL ME HOME AGAIN |

I wake, the sound is gone. I’m sitting up, and something has been picking at my neck. Something with talons and needles, something with a lot of dirt under it’s nails. Something entirely familiar.

| ABSOLUTELY, PLEASE |

Take me, shape my legs until you see what you want to see. Crawl through the wreckage and place your fingers
on my spine, and try to hurt me. Forget the real world, this is all we have, all we need.

It’s gone hollow, all of the sudden. I threw up on myself. I can’t feel my fingers, it’s so cold out. I forget the last thing I said to him. It's weird here, the shadows are playing tricks on me.

| I’M BACK, FOR TONIGHT |

Curses, invocations. I feel a new noise in the air, something with taste, something with substance. I call myself by a new name, and I swirl that around in my mouth. I can be turned over, just like that.

Without warning, I peel back the skin. There is a soot building in my clavicle, I should probably get that removed. Too many people come through here, trying to etch their way through an old story I gave them.
