Hello! My name is Dawson Gyllenhammer, and I’m currently studying Graphic Design at Western Washington University. I love creating custom typography, and building poster designs as personal projects. As of now, I plan to pursue a career in the UI/UX industry, but I also have some interest in branding, as well as product packaging design. I really like developing and refining new skillsets in the larger world of design, so I’m always open to learning new things. In general, I also really love type hierarchy and creating dynamic type systems using grid structures. This industry is so big and varied, and i’m very excited for what the future holds.
